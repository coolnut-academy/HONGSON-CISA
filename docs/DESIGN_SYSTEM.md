# HONGSON-CISA Design System v4.0
## Sapphire-Teal Luxury Research Theme

## üèõÔ∏è Design Philosophy

This system creates a **high-end, research-grade** aesthetic. It moves away from generic "tech" or "warm" vibes to a **Cool, Academic, and Professional** atmosphere suitable for serious assessment and data analysis.

### Core Principles
1. **INTEGRITY** - Academic, high-trust, professional
2. **RESTRAINT** - Color as signal, not decoration
3. **DEPTH** - Subtle surface layering with cool undertones
4. **CLARITY** - High-contrast text driven by cognitive hierarchy
5. **LUXURY** - Refined hues, blue-tinted shadows, premium whites

## üìù Cognitive Hierarchy (Typography)

Text is the **highest visual priority**.

1. **Primary Text** (`#0f172a`): Used for headings, data values, and core content.
2. **Secondary Text** (`#334155`): Used for labels, descriptions, and metadata.
3. **Tertiary Text** (`#64748b`): Used for placeholders and low-priority hints.

*Rule: Never let accent colors overpower the text.*

## üìê Luxury Details

### Blue-Tinted Shadows
We avoid muddy gray shadows. Instead, shadows have a subtle **Deep Teal/Navy** undertone (`rgba(15, 118, 110, 0.04)`), creating a crisp, clean depth that feels expensive.

### Surface Layering
1. **Background** (`#f2f5f8`) - The desk.
2. **Card** (`#ffffff`) - The paper.
3. **Inner Section** (`#f8fafc`) - The organized block.

Allows for complex information density without visual clutter.

---

## üö´ What We Avoided

- **Brown/Sand/Cream**: Too cozy/casual for research software.
- **Neon/Cyberpunk**: Too gamified, distracting.
- **Pure Black**: Too harsh, causes eye strain.
- **Flat Gray**: Too boring, corporate, and "dead".
